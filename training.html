Training

Training Programme

J. V. A. G. Pharma Consultants is engaged in conducting various Training Programmes for Regulatory Affairs for Pharma Students and the Professionals.

The Training programmes are based on the current requirements of the Pharma Companies and Profession.

The students are trained with the updated current knowledge related to CTDs, e-CTDs, DMFs, PSURs, PVP/PVR etc. We help students in understanding the Pharma Regulatory provisions and a keen insight on the recent Pharmaceutical Affairs and Development coupled with the technical know-how for the same.

Students are allowed to travel to the Client's site, R & D and Production site to increase the depth of knowledge of trainees on various aspects of Regulatory Affairs, Technical Documentation, GMP and GLP Norms and Regulations.

J. V. A. G. also participates in the Conferences, Seminars and Exhibitions conducted all over India and Abroad thus giving practical exposure to trainees regarding pharmaceutical updates and regulations.

This kind of training helps the Students and the Regulatory Professionals to gain the requisite Knowledge, sharpening of Skills, changing of Attitude and Behaviour as well as the Personality Development which improves their performance as an individual.

Besides, the company helps Students in providing good placements in the regulatory department at various renowned pharmaceutical companies to pursue the bright future.
